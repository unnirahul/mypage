Cash Flow Statement

Reports the different channels to which changes in cash duiring teh year may be attributed to. The balance sheet tracks the change in a company's cash balance from one year to the next. However it does not tell us why the cash balance changes. Income statement does not track changes to cash as everything recorded on an accrual basis.

Catagories

  1. Cash flow from operating activities
    • Cash flow from operations related current assets and liabilities
  2. Cash flow from investing activties
    • Cash flow from non current assets
  4. Cash flow from financing activities
    • Cash flow from non-current liabilities and shareholder's equity
